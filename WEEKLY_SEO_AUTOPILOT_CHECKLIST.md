## ðŸ’¡ PRO TIPS

### Tip 1: Batch Your SEO Work
Don't do SEO tasks daily. Block 30 minutes every Monday morning for all weekly tasks.

### Tip 2: Focus on 1 Thing Per Week
- Week 1: Backlink outreach
- Week 2: Content creation
- Week 3: Technical SEO
- Week 4: Analytics review

### Tip 3: Automate What You Can
- Use Buffer/Later to schedule social media posts
- Set up Google Alerts for your brand name
- Use Zapier to get weekly GSC reports emailed to you

### Tip 4: Don't Obsess Over Rankings
Rankings fluctuate daily. Focus on trend over 30 days, not daily changes.

### Tip 5: Quality > Quantity
1 high-quality backlink (DA 70+) > 10 low-quality backlinks (DA 20)
1 comprehensive 2000-word blog post > 5 thin 300-word posts
