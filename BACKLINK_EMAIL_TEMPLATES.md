# Backlink Outreach Email Templates

## How to Use These Templates

1. **Find target websites** using Google:
   - Search: "Dubai real estate blog"
   - Search: "UAE property marketing blog"
   - Search: "real estate technology blog"
   - Search: "Dubai startup blog"

2. **Find contact emails:**
   - Check "Contact" page
   - Use Hunter.io (free 25 searches/month)
   - Try: hello@domain.com, info@domain.com, editor@domain.com

3. **Send 5 emails per week** (personalize each one)

4. **Track responses** in Google Sheets

## Tips for Higher Success Rate

1. **Personalize every email:** Mention specific content from their site
2. **Provide value first:** Offer data, insights, or broken link fixes
3. **Keep it short:** 150-200 words max
4. **Follow up once:** If no response in 7 days, send a polite follow-up
5. **Quality over quantity:** 5 personalized emails > 50 generic emails
6. **Build relationships:** Engage with their content on social media first
