The 5-Step Referral System

1

The Right Moment to Ask

Ask at peak happiness: When they've gotten great results. NOT at the beginning, NOT when they're stressed.

Script: "I'm so glad things worked out well for you! I have a favor to ask - do you know anyone else who might need similar help? I'd love to take care of your friends and family the same way I took care of you."
2

Make It Easy With Referral Cards

Give them something tangible. Physical cards get used 3x more than "just tell them about us."

Card Template (Front):
"A friend thought you'd benefit from this."
[Your Name] | [Your Title]
[Phone] | [Website]
Present this card for a complimentary consultation
3

The Thank-You Reward

When a referral books a consultation, immediately thank the referrer:

  • $100-$200 credit toward future services
  • Gift card to a nice restaurant
  • VIP treatment at next interaction
  • Handwritten thank-you note (most powerful!)
4

The Double Reward (When They Convert)

When the referral becomes a paying client, send another reward:

"[Name], I wanted to let you know that [Friend's Name] just signed on! Thank you so much for the introduction. As a token of appreciation, I've [reward]. You're the best! - [Your Name]"
5

Referral Reactivation Email

Send quarterly to past clients:

Subject: Know someone who could use my help?

Hi [Name],

I was thinking about you today and how well things turned out for you! I hope everything is still going great.

Quick question: do you have any friends, family, or colleagues who might be dealing with [problem you solve]? I'd love to help them the same way I helped you.

Just have them mention your name when they reach out, and you'll both receive [reward].

Thank you for being such a wonderful client!
[Your Name]
