Expertise Demonstration

Show what you know through educational content

Content Types:

  • How-to guides and tutorials
  • Common mistake breakdowns
  • Industry myth-busting
  • Process explanations
  • FAQ answer videos

Example Topics:

"The 5 biggest mistakes people make with [topic]"
"What actually happens when you [process]"
"Why [common belief] is completely wrong"
"[Number] things I wish everyone knew about [topic]"

2

Social Proof & Results

Let your results speak for themselves

Content Types:

  • Client success stories (with permission)
  • Before/after transformations
  • Case study breakdowns
  • Results and statistics
  • Testimonial compilations

Storytelling Framework:

1. The Problem: "They came to me with [challenge]..."
2. The Solution: "Here's the approach we took..."
3. The Result: "Now they're [positive outcome]..."
4. The Lesson: "What this teaches us is..."

3

Thought Leadership

Share opinions and perspectives that differentiate you

Content Types:

  • Industry commentary and trends
  • Hot takes and opinions
  • Predictions and forecasts
  • Philosophy and values
  • What you believe differently

Thought Leadership Prompts:

"Most people in my industry believe X. I believe Y. Here's why..."
"The future of [industry] is changing. Here's what I'm seeing..."
"Unpopular opinion: [Controversial but defensible take]"
"If I were starting over today, here's what I'd do differently..."
