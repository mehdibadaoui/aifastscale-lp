Email Template

Handling Negative Reviews

The Golden Rules

  • NEVER respond emotionally or defensively
  • NEVER share private details
  • ALWAYS respond publicly within 24 hours
  • ALWAYS take the conversation offline

Professional Response Template

Thank you for taking the time to share your feedback, [Name]. We take all client experiences seriously and are sorry to hear you're not completely satisfied.

We'd like the opportunity to discuss your concerns and make things right. Please contact us at [phone/email] at your earliest convenience.

- [Your Name] and the [Business] Team

This shows other readers you're professional and care, even when things go wrong.
