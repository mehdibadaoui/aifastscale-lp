The Case Study Framework

Structure every success story for maximum impact

The STAR Method:

  • Situation: What was the client's problem/challenge?
  • Task: What did they need to accomplish?
  • Action: What did you do to help them?
  • Result: What was the outcome? (Use numbers when possible)

Example Template:

"[Client Type] came to us facing [specific challenge]. They needed [outcome] but were struggling with [obstacle].

We [your approach/method] which resulted in [specific result]. Now they're [positive current state]."

Permission & Privacy System

Share success stories ethically and professionally

Always Get Written Permission:

Never share identifying details without explicit consent.

Permission Request Script:

"I'm so proud of what we achieved together, and I'd love to share your story to help others in similar situations. Would you be comfortable if I featured your experience on my website/social media? I can keep it anonymous or use your first name only - whatever you prefer."

When They Say No:

You can still share the story anonymously:
"A recent client..." or "Someone who came to us with..."
Focus on the situation and outcome without identifying details.

4

Testimonial Capture System

Get powerful testimonials systematically

Ask These Questions:

  • "What was your situation before we started working together?"
  • "What results have you seen?"
  • "What would you tell someone considering working with me?"
  • "What surprised you most about the process?"
  • "Would you recommend me to others? Why?"

Best Time to Ask:

Immediately after a big win or positive outcome. The emotion is fresh, and they're most likely to say yes.
