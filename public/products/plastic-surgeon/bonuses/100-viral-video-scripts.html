The "Biggest Mistake" Warning
Education

"The biggest mistake people make when getting a nose job..."

They bring in a photo of someone else's nose and say "I want this exact nose."

Here's the problem: that nose was designed for THAT face. The proportions, the angles, the way it balances with their chin and forehead.

A great rhinoplasty surgeon doesn't copy. They create a nose that's perfect for YOUR unique facial structure. That's why my results look natural - because they're designed specifically for each patient.

Want to see what YOUR ideal nose could look like? We do 3D imaging at every consultation.

04
The "Recovery Truth" Myth-Buster
Myth Buster

"Here's what rhinoplasty recovery ACTUALLY looks like - and it's not as scary as TikTok makes it seem."

Day 1-3: Yes, there's swelling and bruising. You'll look like you got in a fight.

Day 7: Cast comes off. You'll see the new shape but still have swelling.

Day 14: Most bruising is gone. You can wear makeup and go out.

Month 1: 70% of swelling gone. Most people can't tell you had surgery.

Month 3-12: Final result emerges as subtle swelling continues to resolve.

The truth? Most of my patients are back to normal activities in 2 weeks. Comment "RECOVERY" and I'll send you our complete guide.

05
The "Closed vs Open" Explainer
Education

"Open rhinoplasty or closed? Here's how I decide which technique to use..."

CLOSED: All incisions inside the nose. No visible scars. Faster recovery. Great for minor refinements.

OPEN: Small incision under the nose tip. Gives me complete visibility. Better for complex cases, revision surgery, or major reshaping.

I don't pick one technique for everyone. I look at YOUR nose, YOUR goals, and choose the approach that will give YOU the best result.

Want to know which technique would be best for your nose? Let's talk about it in a consultation.

Non-Surgical Scripts

15 viral scripts for injectables & treatments
13
The "Frozen Face" Fix
Myth Buster

"Scared of looking frozen with Botox? Here's how to make sure that NEVER happens..."

The frozen look happens when: 1) Too much is injected, 2) It's placed incorrectly, or 3) The provider doesn't understand facial anatomy.

When done right, Botox should make you look rested and refreshed - not expressionless. You should still be able to show emotion, just without the deep lines.

I always start conservative with new patients. You can add more in 2 weeks. You can't take it away.

Want to try Botox but scared of the frozen look? Start with a consultation. I'll show you exactly what natural results look like.

14
The "Filler Migration" Warning
Education

"Filler migration is real. Here's how to avoid it..."

You've seen the photos - filler moving to places it shouldn't be, creating that "duck lip" or "pillow face" look.

This happens because of: 1) Wrong type of filler for the area, 2) Too much product, 3) Poor injection technique, 4) Getting filler too frequently.

The key is less is more, the right product for each area, and giving time between treatments. Overfilled faces happen from rushing results.

Want filler done right? Come see someone who knows when to say "that's enough." Book via the link.
