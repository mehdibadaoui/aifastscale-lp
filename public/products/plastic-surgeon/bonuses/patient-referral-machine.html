The 5-Step Referral System

1

The Right Moment to Ask

Ask at peak happiness: 3-6 months post-op when they're loving their results. NOT at checkout, NOT immediately after surgery.

Script: "You look absolutely amazing! I love seeing patients this happy with their results. I have a favor to ask - do you know anyone else who might be interested in a similar transformation? I'd love to take care of your friends and family the same way I took care of you."
2

Make It Easy With Referral Cards

Give them something tangible to hand out. Physical cards get used 3x more than "just tell them about us."

3

The Thank-You Reward

When a referral books a consultation, immediately thank the referrer:

  • $100-$200 credit toward future treatments
  • Free Botox touch-up or skincare product
  • VIP treatment at next appointment
  • Handwritten thank-you note (most powerful!)
4

The Double Reward (When They Convert)

When the referral actually books a procedure, send another reward:

"[Name], I wanted to let you know that [Friend's Name] just scheduled their surgery! Thank you so much for sending them our way. As a token of appreciation, I've added a $300 credit to your account for your next treatment. You're the best! - Dr. [Name]"
5
