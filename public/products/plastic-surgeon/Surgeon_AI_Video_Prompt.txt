================================================================================
                              THE PROMPT
================================================================================

Create a professional, realistic talking head video of a plastic surgeon.

IDENTITY PRESERVATION (CRITICAL)
--------------------------------
- The face MUST remain 100% identical to the reference image
- Preserve exact facial features, bone structure, skin texture, age, ethnicity
- No beautification, no filters, no AI-smoothing
- Natural human imperfections must be visible


PROFESSIONAL AESTHETIC
----------------------
- Setting: Modern, clean clinic or professional office
- Attire: Professional medical attire (scrubs or white coat)
- Lighting: Soft, natural lighting - no harsh shadows
- Background: Subtle, non-distracting (blurred clinic interior)


SPEAKING STYLE
--------------
- Direct eye contact with camera
- Confident but approachable demeanor
- Natural head movements and micro-expressions
- Professional yet warm communication style
- Speaking to a potential patient, not reading a script


TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS
----------------------
- 4K resolution or highest available
- 16:9 aspect ratio
- Natural lip sync with audio
- Subtle natural movements (blinks, micro-expressions)
- Duration: Match audio length exactly


WHAT TO AVOID
-------------
- Uncanny valley effects
- Robotic or artificial movements
- Over-smooth skin (plastic look)
- Exaggerated expressions
- Stiff or frozen body language
