### The 5 Reasons People DO Refer: 1. **Reciprocity** - You've given them value, they want to give back 2. **Social Currency** - Recommending you makes THEM look good 3. **Identity** - They see themselves as helpful connectors 4. **Relationship** - They genuinely want to help you succeed 5. **Incentive** - There's something (even small) in it for them ### The 5 Reasons People DON'T Refer: 1. **Forgot** - You're not top of mind when opportunity arises 2. **Unsure** - They don't know exactly who to refer or how 3. **Risk** - They're afraid the referral won't go well 4. **Unasked** - You never actually asked them 5. **No System** - It's too complicated to refer **Your system must address ALL of these.**

ASK: Request Referrals Strategically
### The Perfect Timing **Best moments to ask:** - After a major success/positive outcome - When they express gratitude or satisfaction - At the natural end of service - During a check-in when they report great results - After they've referred you to someone else (meta!) **Worst moments to ask:** - During service delivery (they're focused on themselves) - When there's any unresolved issue - Out of the blue with no context - When they're clearly busy or stressed ### The Referral Ask Framework **Step 1: Set the Stage (Seed the idea early)** > "I love working with clients like you. Most of my best clients come from referrals‚Äîso if you know anyone who might benefit from what we do, I'd love to help them too." **Step 2: Make the Ask (Be specific)** > "Do you know anyone who's dealing with [specific problem you solved for them]? Even if you're not sure, I'd be happy to have a conversation with them." **Step 3: Make It Easy** > "Just send them my info or text me their name, and I'll reach out personally. I'll make sure they're taken care of."
