--- ## The Offer Stack Framework
1
The Core Offer
This is your main service‚Äîwhat you're actually selling. **Template:** - **Name:** [Outcome-Focused Name] - **What it is:** [Clear description of the service] - **Who it's for:** [Specific target client] - **The transformation:** From [before] ‚Üí To [after] - **Timeline:** [How long it takes] **Example:** - **Name:** The Client Magnet System - **What it is:** A done-with-you content creation program - **Who it's for:** Professionals who want to attract clients with video - **Transformation:** From invisible online ‚Üí To booking calls from content - **Timeline:** 90 days to first results
2
The Bonus Stack
Bonuses increase perceived value without increasing your cost. ### Types of Bonuses: - **Information:** Templates, scripts, guides - **Access:** Group calls, community, you - **Tools:** Software, resources, shortcuts - **Fast-start:** Quickstart materials to get wins early ### Bonus Rules: - Each bonus should have a clear value ($ amount) - Bonuses should solve adjacent problems - Total bonus value should exceed core offer price - Include "surprise" bonuses to exceed expectations **Example Bonus Stack:** | Bonus | Value | |-------|-------| | 100 Viral Video Scripts | $497 | | AI Content Generator Prompts | $397 | | Weekly Group Coaching Calls | $297 | | Private Community Access | $197 | | Phone Scripts That Book | $197 | | **Total Bonus Value** | **$1,585** |
3
The Guarantee
Guarantees remove risk and increase conversion. ### Types of Guarantees: **Unconditional:** > "If you're not happy for any reason within 30 days, get a full refund." **Conditional:** > "Complete the program, implement the strategies, and if you don't see results within 90 days, I'll work with you for free until you do." **Outcome-Based:** > "If you don't [specific result] within [timeframe], I'll refund 100%." ### Choose Based On: - Your confidence in results - Your ability to deliver - Client commitment requirements
4
Urgency & Scarcity
People need a reason to act NOW. ### Legitimate Urgency: - **Time-based:** "Price goes up Friday at midnight" - **Quantity-based:** "Only 5 spots available this month" - **Bonus-based:** "These bonuses are only available until [date]" - **Season-based:** "Before the new year / summer / etc." ### Rules: - Must be TRUE (fake scarcity destroys trust) - Must be CLEAR (specific deadlines) - Must be ENFORCED (actually raise prices, remove bonuses)

--- ## The 5 Simple Funnels
1
The Direct Booking Funnel
**Best for:** High-ticket services, consultations **The Flow:** ``` Content ‚Üí Profile/Bio ‚Üí Calendar Link ‚Üí Consultation ‚Üí Sale ``` **What You Need:** - Clear call-to-action in all content - Optimized bio with one link - Calendar scheduling tool (Calendly, Cal.com) - Consultation script **Example:** Video ends with "Link in bio to book a free call" ‚Üí Bio has Calendly link ‚Üí They book ‚Üí You close on the call
2
The Lead Magnet Funnel
**Best for:** Building an email list, nurturing leads **The Flow:** ``` Content ‚Üí Free Resource ‚Üí Email Sequence ‚Üí Offer ‚Üí Sale ``` **What You Need:** - Valuable free resource (PDF, video, template) - Simple landing page - Email follow-up sequence - Offer presentation **Example:** Video mentions "Free guide: 10 Scripts That Convert" ‚Üí Landing page collects email ‚Üí 5-email sequence delivers value ‚Üí Final email presents offer
3
The DM Funnel
**Best for:** Personal touch, high-engagement platforms **The Flow:** ``` Content ‚Üí Comment "X" ‚Üí DM Conversation ‚Üí Qualify ‚Üí Book Call ``` **What You Need:** - Engaging content with clear CTA - DM scripts - Qualification questions - Transition to call **Example:** Post says "Comment 'VIDEO' for my free strategy" ‚Üí They comment ‚Üí You DM with the resource ‚Üí Ask qualifying questions ‚Üí Invite to call if good fit
4
The Webinar Funnel
**Best for:** Education-based selling, group presentations **The Flow:** ``` Ad/Content ‚Üí Registration ‚Üí Webinar ‚Üí Offer ‚Üí Sale ``` **What You Need:** - Webinar topic that attracts ideal clients - Registration page - Presentation slides - Offer presentation at the end **Example:** Ad for "Free Training: How to Get Clients from Video" ‚Üí They register ‚Üí Attend live or watch replay ‚Üí You present offer at the end
5
The Low-Ticket Funnel
**Best for:** Self-qualifying buyers, scaling **The Flow:** ``` Content ‚Üí Low-Ticket Offer ‚Üí Delivery ‚Üí Upsell to High-Ticket ``` **What You Need:** - Low-priced digital product ($7-$97) - Simple checkout page - Delivery system - Upsell sequence **Example:** Sell a $47 mini-course ‚Üí Buyers get access ‚Üí Email sequence offers 1:1 consulting ‚Üí Serious buyers book calls
