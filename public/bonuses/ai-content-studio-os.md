# MODULE 1.1: BRAND VOICE DEVELOPMENT

## Creating Your Unique Content DNA

### The Voice Framework

Your voice = Your personality on content.

**The 5 Voice Dimensions:**

1. **Tone** - How you feel (serious, playful, warm, direct)
2. **Style** - How you express (storyteller, teacher, challenger)
3. **Energy** - Your intensity (calm, energetic, passionate)
4. **Vocabulary** - Words you use (simple, technical, conversational)
5. **Perspective** - Your stance (authoritative, approachable, provocative)

---

### Voice Discovery Exercise

Complete these sentences:

**I am the [professional type] who:**
_________________________________

**My content makes people feel:**
_________________________________

**If my brand was a person, they would be:**
_________________________________

**I believe that [industry topic] should be:**
_________________________________

**My content is NOT [what competitors do]:**
_________________________________

---

### Video Script Prompt Templates

**Hook Video Script:**
```
Create a 30-second video script about [topic] for a
[profession] targeting [audience].

Requirements:
- Hook in first 3 seconds (pattern interrupt)
- One clear point or question
- Creates curiosity to watch more
- Ends with micro-CTA (comment, follow, watch next)

Format:
[HOOK]: What to say/show
[BODY]: The content
[CTA]: The ask
```

**Tip Video Script:**
```
Create a 60-second tip video script teaching [topic].

Structure:
- Hook: Why this matters (5 sec)
- Context: The problem (10 sec)
- Tip: What to do (30 sec)
- Result: What happens (10 sec)
- CTA: What to do next (5 sec)

Tone: [Your brand voice]
Include: Visual suggestions for AI avatar
```

**Story Video Script:**
```
Create a 2-minute story video about [transformation/
experience/lesson].

Arc:
- Opening: Set the scene (15 sec)
- Conflict: The challenge (30 sec)
- Journey: What happened (45 sec)
- Resolution: The outcome (20 sec)
- Lesson: The takeaway (10 sec)

Make it emotional and specific.
```

**Educational Video Script:**
```
Create a 5-minute educational video about [topic].

Outline:
1. Hook + Promise (30 sec)
2. Why This Matters (45 sec)
3. Main Point 1 (60 sec)
4. Main Point 2 (60 sec)
5. Main Point 3 (60 sec)
6. Summary + CTA (45 sec)

Include: Examples, visuals suggestions, transitions
```

### A/B Testing Process

1. **Hypothesis:** "If I [change], then [result]"
2. **Test:** Create two versions
3. **Measure:** Compare performance
4. **Analyze:** What does data show?
5. **Implement:** Use winner going forward
6. **Repeat:** Continuous improvement
