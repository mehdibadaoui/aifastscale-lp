--- ## The Psychology of Case Studies
üß† Why Case Studies Convert
**The Trust Hierarchy:** 1. **They say it themselves** (testimonial) = Maximum trust 2. **You tell their story** (case study) = Very high trust 3. **You claim it** (marketing copy) = Low trust **Case studies work because they:** - Show proof, not promises - Let prospects see themselves in the story - Answer objections before they arise - Create emotional connection - Provide specific, believable results
--- ## The Universal Case Study Structure
üìã The PAOR Framework
Every great case study follows this structure: **P** - Problem (What they were struggling with) **A** - Approach (What you did to help) **O** - Outcome (The results they achieved) **R** - Reflection (What they say now) Use this framework for ANY format‚Äîwritten, video, carousel, or social post.
