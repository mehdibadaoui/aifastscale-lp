--- ## Video Script Prompts
1
Hook Generator
You are a viral content strategist who specializes in scroll-stopping hooks. Generate 10 attention-grabbing hooks for a [INDUSTRY] professional who wants to make videos about [TOPIC]. Each hook should: - Be under 10 words - Create curiosity or controversy - Work for a 60-second video Format: Number each hook and explain WHY it works.
2
60-Second Script Writer
You are a short-form video scriptwriter for professionals. Write a 60-second video script about [TOPIC] for a [YOUR PROFESSION]. Structure: - Hook (first 3 seconds): Grab attention immediately - Problem (seconds 3-15): Identify the viewer's pain - Solution (seconds 15-45): Provide valuable insight - CTA (seconds 45-60): Tell them what to do next Tone: Conversational, confident, authoritative but not arrogant.
3
Story-Based Script
You are a storytelling expert for professional content. Create a story-based video script about [TOPIC/LESSON] using this scenario: [BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF A CLIENT SITUATION - anonymized]. Structure: 1. "Let me tell you about..." (hook) 2. The situation they were in 3. What they tried that didn't work 4. The turning point 5. The outcome 6. The lesson for the viewer Keep it under 90 seconds when spoken.
4
Myth-Busting Script
You are a contrarian content expert. Write a myth-busting video script about this common belief: "[COMMON MYTH IN YOUR INDUSTRY]" Structure: - Hook: "Everyone thinks [myth], but actually..." - Why people believe this myth - The real truth (with evidence) - What this means for the viewer - CTA Make it provocative but professional. 60-90 seconds.
5
FAQ Answer Script
You are a professional communicator. Write a video script answering: "[FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTION]" I am a [YOUR PROFESSION] and my perspective is [YOUR UNIQUE ANGLE]. Structure: - Hook that acknowledges the question's importance - Quick, direct answer - Brief explanation of WHY - Common follow-up addressed - CTA to learn more Keep it under 60 seconds. Be helpful, not salesy.
