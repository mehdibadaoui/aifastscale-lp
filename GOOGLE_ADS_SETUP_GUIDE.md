## üéØ PHASE 2: Link GA4 to Google Ads (Do This in 24-48 Hours)

### Step 1: Link GA4 Property to Google Ads

1. **Go to Google Analytics**: https://analytics.google.com
2. Click **Admin** (bottom left gear icon)
3. In the **Property** column (middle), click **Google Ads Links**
4. Click **Link** button (blue button at top right)
5. **Select your Google Ads account** from the list
   - Should show: Account ID ending in `7512`
6. Click **Next**
7. Toggle **ON** the data stream: "AI Fast Scale Website"
8. Click **Next**
9. Under "Link Configuration":
   - Toggle **ON**: "Enable personalized advertising"
   - Toggle **ON**: "Enable auto-tagging"
10. Click **Submit**

**Result**: GA4 is now linked to Google Ads! ‚úÖ

---

### Step 2: Import "Purchase" Conversion to Google Ads

1. **Go to Google Ads**: https://ads.google.com
2. Click **Goals** in left menu ‚Üí **Conversions**
3. Click **+ New conversion action** (blue button)
4. Select **Import** ‚Üí **Google Analytics 4 properties**
5. Select **Web** as the source
6. You should see your GA4 property listed
7. Find the **"purchase"** event and check the box
8. Click **Import and continue**
9. **Configure conversion settings**:
   - **Goal**: Purchase
   - **Value**: Use value from purchase event (should be $37)
   - **Count**: Every conversion
   - **Conversion window**: 30 days
   - **View-through conversion window**: 1 day
   - **Attribution model**: Data-driven (or Last click if data-driven not available)
10. Click **Done**

**Result**: Purchase conversions will now flow from GA4 ‚Üí Google Ads! ‚úÖ

---

### Step 3: Mark "Purchase" as Primary Conversion

1. Still in **Goals ‚Üí Conversions** page
2. Find your "purchase" conversion in the list
3. Click the **Primary action** column dropdown
4. Select **Primary**
5. Click **Save**

**Why?**: This tells Google Ads to optimize your campaigns for purchases (not just clicks).

## üìä PHASE 4: Monitor & Optimize (First 7 Days)

### Daily Checklist

**Day 1-3**: Learning Phase
- Check Google Ads dashboard daily
- Look at: Impressions, Clicks, CTR, Cost
- **Don't make changes yet!** Let Google learn

**Day 4-7**: Initial Optimization
- Pause keywords with 0 impressions
- Increase bids on keywords with clicks but no conversions
- Add negative keywords for irrelevant searches
- Check GA4 for "purchase" events

### Key Metrics to Track

**Google Ads**:
- Impressions: 500-2,000/day (goal)
- Clicks: 10-40/day (goal)
- CTR: 3-8% (good)
- CPC: $0.50-2.00 (typical for UAE)
- Conversions: 3-5/week (goal at $10-20/day budget)

**Google Analytics 4**:
- Sessions: Check if traffic is coming from Google Ads
- Purchase events: Should match Google Ads conversions
- Conversion rate: 2-5% is great for $37 product

### When to Scale

**After 7 days with good results**:
- If you get 3+ sales at profitable ROI
- Increase budget by 20% every 3 days
- Example: $10/day ‚Üí $12/day ‚Üí $14/day
- Goal: Reach $50-100/day with 2x-3x ROI

## üö® Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. ‚ùå **Running ads before GA4 is linked**: Always link GA4 first!
2. ‚ùå **Not waiting for learning phase**: Give campaigns 7 days minimum
3. ‚ùå **Too many keywords**: Start with 5-10, expand later
4. ‚ùå **Using Broad match**: Stick to Phrase and Exact match
5. ‚ùå **No negative keywords**: You'll waste money on irrelevant clicks
6. ‚ùå **Changing bids daily**: Be patient, optimize weekly
7. ‚ùå **No conversion tracking**: You'll fly blind without data
8. ‚ùå **Targeting worldwide**: Start with UAE, expand when profitable
